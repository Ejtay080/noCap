About Us

What We Do.

Our goal is to innovate and create new video games that go beyond the industry idea that has been molded for the past decades. We love to inspire and influence art and love of the craft itself. We want to make something incredible and beautiful whilst learning more and more. DayDream Games wants to best all of the awards and achievements by exceeding and subverting the expectations that have been set for the community of video games.


Why We Do.

Inspired by many of the Classics, DayDream Games wants to create a new generation of endless classics. Super Mario Bros. on the Nintendo DS was one of the first games the CEO, Ethan Tayloe, ever played. It changed his perspective on life and where he was heading. He joined a program allowing him to meet the CFO, Evan Kinsler. Together they are developing one of the biggest project DayDream Games has to offer, The Multiverse. They are hard at work, creating every type of innovative, creative, and entertaining gameplay, narrative, and art style imaginable.
