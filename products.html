Heavily inspired by the classic FPS games we all know and love, Alone Escape (including it's single player campaign) takes notes from these original's while creating unorthodox and creative ways on how to make the game fresh and fun! Alone Escape is a single-player, local Co-op, online Co-op, and massive multiplayer game based on the concepts of teleportation. Each player is given a grenade, when thrown, teleports the player to that location. There are certain walls that a cooked grenade cannot be thrown on and on certain walls when cooked correctly, it can teleport a group or a single enemy! We will have many variations of the combat and modes, including our own CTF based mode called Interruption, where groups of teams work together to plan and use travel grenades accordingly to coordinate a plan to Interrupt the Signal on the enemy team. Each signal's goal is to run for 2 minutes successfully without other teams interrupting the signal, so that extraction can come and save the soldiers! Modes like that are heavy thought out and we promise, Alone Escape is an idea that we don't plan on wasting.
